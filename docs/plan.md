# Alfaazi — Shayari & Poetry Studio

## Plan

### Concept
Alfaazi is a professional shayari and poetry creation studio. It's where poets, shayari enthusiasts, and content creators come to write, design, and share beautiful Urdu/Hindi poetry. Think "Canva meets Medium, purpose-built for South Asian poetry."

### The Problem
- Millions share shayari on Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook — but they use generic text-on-image tools
- No dedicated platform understands the nuances of Urdu/Hindi poetry (right-to-left script, nastaliq calligraphy, specific meters like beher)
- Poetry collections are scattered across screenshots, WhatsApp forwards, and Instagram saves
- Content creators spend hours making shayari graphics in Canva with no poetry-specific templates

### Target Audience
1. **Shayari Content Creators** — Instagram pages with 10K-1M+ followers sharing daily shayari posts
2. **Poets & Writers** — People who write their own shayari/ghazals/nazms
3. **Poetry Enthusiasts** — Millions who consume and share shayari daily
4. **Urdu Literature Students** — Studying poetry, need organized collections
5. **Wedding/Event Planners** — Need beautiful Urdu calligraphy for invites

### Market Size
- 500M+ Hindi speakers, 230M+ Urdu speakers
- #shayari has 10M+ posts on Instagram
- #urdushayari has 5M+ posts
- Shayari WhatsApp groups have millions of active users
- Zero dedicated SaaS tools exist

### Revenue Model
- **Free:** 5 designs/month, basic templates, watermark
- **Creator ($29/mo):** Unlimited designs, premium templates, no watermark, transliteration, HD export
- **Studio ($79/mo):** Everything + brand kits, team collaboration, API, bulk export, analytics

### Competitors
- Canva (generic, no poetry-specific features)
- Various shayari apps (low quality, ad-infested, content only — no creation tools)
- Instagram itself (but no dedicated poetry tools)
- **No direct SaaS competitor**

### Tech Stack
- React + TypeScript + Vite
- TailwindCSS v4
- Lucide Icons
- Google Fonts (Noto Nastaliq Urdu, Amiri, plus Latin display fonts)
- GitHub Pages (static marketing site + interactive demo)
